UI/UX Guidelines for Checkout Page

1. Color Scheme
   - Primary Button (Apply, Pay Now): Blue (#007bff) or Green (#28a745) for success actions.
   - Secondary Button (Remove): Grey (#6c757d).
   - Background: Light Grey (#f4f4f4).
   - Container: White (#fff) with shadow.

2. Form Validation
   - Error messages must be displayed in RED text immediately below the invalid field.
   - Required fields: Name, Email, Address.
   - Email must contain '@' symbol.
   - "Pay Now" button should trigger validation.

3. Feedback
   - Successful discount application: Green text "15% Discount Applied!".
   - Invalid discount: Red text "Invalid Discount Code".
   - Successful Payment: Replace form with Green text "Payment Successful!".

4. Layout
   - Cart summary at the top.
   - Shipping details and payment form below.
   - Total price must be clearly visible and update dynamically.
